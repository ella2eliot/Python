分群結果解釋¶

透過學生的學術背景作為分群的特徵，包含 先前學歷成績、入學成績，共分成三群，根據群體特徵分析推斷

  • 第一群(0):顯示該群組是數據的主要密度區域。
  • 第二、三群(1, 2):是小型群體，可能代表成績特別高或低的學生群體，可能是學校使用不同錄取政策（如特殊招生）。
  • 其他(黑點):表明一些數據點與其他群體差異明顯，這可能是成績異常的學生。學術基礎好（X 軸值高）的學生，通常錄取分數（Y 軸值）也高。

Using students' academic background as clustering features, including previous qualification grades and admission grades, the data is divided into three groups. Based on the characteristics of each group, the analysis suggests:

  • First Group (0): Represents the primary density area of the data.
  • Second and Third Groups (1, 2): These are smaller groups, potentially representing students with particularly high or low grades, which may result from the school applying different admission policies (e.g., special admissions).
  • Others (Black Points): Indicate data points that differ significantly from other groups. These may correspond to students with anomalous grades. Students with a strong academic foundation (high X-axis values) are generally associated with higher admission grades (high Y-axis values).
