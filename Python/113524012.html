分群結果解釋：¶

透過學生的學術背景作為分群的特徵，包含 先前學歷成績、入學成績、第一學期成績、第二學期成績、第一學期通過課程數、第二學期通過課程數。 共分成三群，根據群體特徵分析推斷

  • 第一群(0)：入學成績和先前學歷成績中等，學術基礎適中。第一、二學期的課程成績良好（平均約 12.34），課程通過數也處於中等偏高水平（約 5 門和 4.9 門）。大多數的學生都被歸類在這一群，是表現穩定但不特別頂尖突出的學生。
  • 第二群(1)：入學成績和先前學歷成績略高於平均。但第一、二學期的課程成績極低（平均 2.45 和 0.52），幾乎沒有通過課程（平均約 0.5 門和 0.08 門）。是學生中的少數群體，雖然學術背景良好，但可能受外部因素導致學習困難的學生。
  • 第三群(2)：入學成績和先前學歷成績顯著高於其他群。第一、二學期的課程成績非常高（平均 13.25 和 13.20），且通過課程數最多（平均約 6.6 門和 6.4 門）。是標準的高成就學習者。

Explanation of Clustering Results:¶

The clustering was based on students' academic backgrounds, including Previous qualification (grade), Admission grade, Curricular units 1st sem (grade), Curricular units 2nd sem (grade), Curricular units 1st sem (approved), Curricular units 2nd sem (approved).

The students were divided into three groups, and the characteristics of each group were analyzed as follows:

  • Group 0: This group exhibits moderate entrance scores and prior academic performance, indicating an average academic foundation. Their course grades in the first and second semesters are relatively good (averaging around 12.34), and the number of courses passed is slightly above average (approximately 5 courses in the first semester and 4.9 in the second semester). Most students fall into this group, representing those with stable but not exceptionally outstanding performance.

  • Group 1: This group shows slightly above-average entrance scores and prior academic performance. However, their first- and second-semester course grades are extremely low (averaging 2.45 and 0.52, respectively), with almost no courses passed (around 0.5 courses in the first semester and 0.08 in the second semester). This is a minority group of students who, despite having a strong academic background, may face significant external challenges that hinder their academic performance.

  • Group 2: Students in this group have significantly higher entrance scores and prior academic performance than those in other groups. Their first- and second-semester course grades are exceptionally high (averaging 13.25 and 13.20, respectively), and they pass the highest number of courses (around 6.6 courses in the first semester and 6.4 in the second semester). This group represents the standard high-achieving learners.
